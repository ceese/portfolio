Creative Director

11/2008 - 02/2015: Cray, Inc.; Seattle, WA

I was responsible for the visual branding of Cray and overseeing to the company’s marketing material needs. In addition to setting the standards and processes for the design department, I worked with product managers and other stake holders to oversee the visual design, content development, information architecture, layout and production of marketing materials. These included web sites, brochures, print and banner ads, landing pages, marketing emails, social media, trade show booth graphics, interactive kiosks and video.
  • Created new visual identity standards for Cray that consolidated, evolved and strengthened the Cray Brand. This included creating the visual identity and writing the standards for Cray’s family of products and new Cray subsidy companies as they were created.
  • Redefined Cray’s visual identity and set the visual and messaging standards for Cray’s marketing materials.
  • Developed new design processes and templates for use within the design group that reduced design and production turnaround time across channels.
  • Created RFPs, set budgets and managed external contractors and projects.
  • Assisted the sales teams to design and layout of sales tools, presentations and client response documents.
